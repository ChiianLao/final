I think of my installations as an open-ended storytelling, where I put together with photographs, painting, and technology in one space.
They are improvisational sites in which the constructed and the readymade are used to question our making of the world through language and knowledge.
My arrangements are schematic, inviting the viewer to get involved into the space of speculation. The work so far has used the idea of propose a story of humanity, and moving forward of struggling with the stereotype and hidden stories. Which I have found some interesting stories by stepping out and actually walked down the street to interview the people, and took a snap of their facial expression. The works that I have created are depended on humanity of the society, in hope to make a clear overlook from the other side of the world.
